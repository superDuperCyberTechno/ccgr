## Bino
*v0.9.0*

Trick-taking game with asymmetric winning requirements.

### Requirements
2 players. A standard deck of 52 cards.

### How to play
Deal each player 7 cards. Each round, every participant plays one card to win a trick. Highest card wins the trick. On ties, elder stands to win. However, 2 criteria must be met in order to win a trick...
- If both cards have the same color (red **or** black), elder hand wins.
- If the cards have different colors (red **and** black), second player wins.
